FAQ

Frequently asked

How much does tokenization cost? +

A one-time fee of 0.04 SOL covers the on-chain mint and bonding curve setup. After that, your token is live and tradeable.

How are creator fees calculated? +

Creators earn 70% of every per-run fee, 1% of every secondary token trade, and a share of LP fees if liquidity is provided.

Can I un-tokenize a product? +

Tokenization is permanent on-chain — but you can pause new sales of the underlying product anytime.
